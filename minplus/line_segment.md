Calculates the points belonging to a tropical line segment given as two points.

Parameters
: `start_point : Collection`: the starting point of the line.
: `end_point : Collection`: the ending point of the line.
: `sort : bool`: a flag determining whether or not to order the points as they appear visually. Defaults to True.
: `unique : bool`: a flag determining whether or not to remove repeating entries from calculations. Defaults to True.

Returns
: `result : numpy.ndarray`: the array of points building the line.
